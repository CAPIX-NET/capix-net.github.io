# ANZSRC 2020 — Socio-Economic Objectives (SEO)

The Socio-Economic Objectives (SEO) classification allows R&D activity to be categorised according to the intended purpose or outcome of the research, rather than the methodology used.

> **How this is used in the RDTI form.** The R&D Tax Incentive registration form asks for the ANZSRC **Division** and **Group** that best describe the majority of R&D activities in each project. Field-level codes are listed here for keyword searching and to help select the correct Group.

> **Source:** Australian and New Zealand Standard Research Classification (ANZSRC) 2020, Catalogue 1297.0, Australian Bureau of Statistics. © Commonwealth of Australia 2020 / © Crown Copyright New Zealand 2020.
