When you lodge your income tax return, complete the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) R&D Tax Incentive schedule and include your Registration Number provided above on this schedule.

Please note that the R&D Tax Incentive program is a self-assessment program. This means that registrants are responsible for ensuring they meet the eligibility criteria of the program regarding registered R&D activities and related expenditure. Registration does not imply eligibility of your R&D activities and it is not an indication of compliance with other R&D Tax Incentive requirements. Consequently, you are required to keep records and evidence to support your registration and claim. Using the services of a R&D tax adviser to assist with the preparation of a registration application and tax offset claim does not guarantee eligibility.

The R&D Tax Incentive program is jointly administered by the Department on behalf of Industry, Innovation and Science Australia and the ATO. The Department undertakes a range of activities to ensure the integrity of the program, including education, guidance, reviews and compliance activities. These may occur after your activities are registered. Should this occur, we will contact you to discuss the registration and we may ask you to provide any evidence used in your self-assessment. The ATO, being responsible for the administration and processing of R&D offset claims, may also contact you to discuss your registration separately. The Department and the ATO work closely together and actively share information about registrations under this co-administration model.

If you have any questions about your registration, please email rdtaxincentive@industry.gov.au or call the business.gov.au Contact Centre on **13 28 46**.
